Vancouver Christmas Market Outing

Dec 06, 2025

On December 6th, our Senior Mental Enrichment Program visited the 2025 Vancouver Christmas Market, enjoying a festive and culturally rich holiday outing. Participants were immersed in the lively atmosphere, where a variety of Christmas performances took place. A highlight of the visit was the impressive 35-foot-tall Christmas tree, beautifully decorated with sparkling lights and serving as a popular spot for photos. Seniors explored over 100 European-style wooden artisan huts, thoughtfully arranged and filled with handcrafted gifts, Christmas decorations, and unique seasonal items. For many participants, this was their first time visiting a German/Austrian Christmas market, so it was very exciting for them. The group enjoyed browsing for special presents to bring home and share the warmth of the holidays with loved ones.
